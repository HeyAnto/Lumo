Lumo: Simplify your Tasks, Stay Focused

Lumo is a minimalist task management app designed to keep you on track with ease and clarity. It’s all about reducing complexity and helping you focus on what’s important, without distractions.

No Setup Needed

Lumo requires no accounts or logins. All your tasks are safely stored directly on your device, and will remain there until you choose to delete them or reset your browser.

Made for You

Lumo is designed to make task management a stress-free experience, helping you prioritize and complete your work in a calm, focused environment.
